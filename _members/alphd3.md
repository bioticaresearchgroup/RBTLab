- Senior research Fellow 

Dr. Sirajunnisa was a senior research fellow (SRF) in Prof. G. S. Sailaja’s research group at the Department of Polymer Science and Rubber Technology, joined in September 2016 as a UGC – Junior Research Fellow. She pursued her master’s degree in Applied Chemistry in 2015 from the Department of Chemistry, University of Calicut, Kerala. Her research works are focused on the development of fluorescent metal organic frameworks and nanohybrids for drug delivery, cellular imaging/ sensing applications.

- Research Interests

Fluorescent organic-inorganic nanohybrids, Cellular imaging/sensing, Controlled drug delivery.
