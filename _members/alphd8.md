Dr. Liz Hannah George joined the lab in September 2016. She has MSc in Applied Chemistry and an M.Phil in Chemistry. Her research is design and synthesis of Metal Organic Frameworks and nanoconjugate systems for Biomedical Applications. She specializes in Synthetic Chemistry; MOFs, Organic-inorganic Hybrids, Cell culture and Biofunctional assays.
