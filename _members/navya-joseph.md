- University Postdoctoral Fellow

Dr. Navya Joseph joined as a University Postdoctoral Fellow in Prof. G.S. Sailaja's team at the Department of Polymer Science and Rubber Technology (PSRT), Cochin University of Science and Technology (CUSAT), in March 2026. She obtained her Ph.D. in Physics from the Department of Physics, CUSAT, in 2024. Her current research focuses on spinel ferrite-based systems for magnetic hyperthermia applications.
